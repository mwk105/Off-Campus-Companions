Tips for Finding a Roommate

  • Communicate openly about your expectations and preferences.
  • Set clear boundaries and discuss responsibilities.
  • Consider lifestyle compatibility, such as study habits and cleanliness.
  • Utilize social media and campus resources to find potential roommates.
  • Meet potential roommates in person to gauge compatibility.
